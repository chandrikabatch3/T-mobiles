Realme gt5 pro

Realme’s China President, Xu Qi, has begun unveiling enticing details about the highly anticipated 
   flagship phone, the Realme GT5 Pro. The upcoming device is set to impress tech enthusiasts with its 
   remarkable specifications, hinting at a new era in smartphone capabilities.Teasing the audience, the
   recent disclosures highlight some of the GT5 Pro’s exceptional features. Among these revelations,
   the phone boasts an enormous 1TB storage capacity, a periscope telephoto lens, and an astonishing
3000 nits brightness display, promising an enhanced visual experience.

Realme c67

The Realme C67 is currently listed on a microsite on the Realme website. The smartphone was also teased
 by the company on X. The teasers reveal that the smartphone, very similar to the Realme Narzo 60x,
 will feature a round camera module at the rear, which will be placed on the top left of the device.
 The teaser also shows a dual-camera setup.The teased image shows off a lime green variant with some
 gradient texture.

Realme Gt Neo 6

According to the tipster on Weibo, the Realme GT Neo 6 will come with the Qualcomm Snapdragon 8 Gen 2 SoC,
 which is still quite powerful.The handset is tipped to feature an aluminium frame, which should make the
 phone quite durable. The tipster claims the Realme GT Neo 6 will be priced under RMB 2,000 (approx Rs 22,900).
 This is a really aggressive pricing for the choice of SoC.There are chances the Realme GT Neo 6 could be based 
 on the Realme GT 5, which launched in China a while ago.

Realme v50s

Realme appears to be working on two new V-series phones, dubbed the Realme V50 and V50s and these have
 now made an appearance on China Telecom. The listing reveals the prices, specifications and design through
 renders. The phones bear the model numbers RMX3783 and RMX3781. Realme’s V-series has mostly remained exclusive
 to China and we expect the same for the Realme V50 as well.
